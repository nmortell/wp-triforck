<div style="clear:both;">
    <h1>Creating Ads</h1>
    <p style="padding-left: 12px;">When you installed AdPress a new sidebar menu for AdPress Ads was created.  
    Select "Create New Ad" from the menu and fill out the form:
    </p>
    <ul style="padding-left: 24px;">
       <li>Title - for your organizational purposes only.</li>
       <li>Destination - the URL where you want the image to link to, blank means no link.</li>
       <li>Featured Image - set featured image, the image to be used for the ad.</li>
    </ul>
    <p style="padding-left: 12px;"><strong>Note: when setting the feature image you must click "set feature image" on the media manager interface.</strong></p>
    <p style="padding-left: 12px;">After you setup the ad, press "publish" to save the ad and make it available to your pages, posts, widgets, and mobile apps.</p>

    <h1>Putting Ads In Posts</h1>
    
    <h2>The Basic Shortcode</h2>
    <p style="padding-left: 12px;">Go to your page or past where you want to show an ad.  Enter the [adpress] shortcode.</p>  
    <p style="padding-left: 12px;">You'll probably not want to always show the first ad. Select others like this:<br/>
    <pre>
    [adpress label="TheBigAd"]
    </pre>
    </p>
